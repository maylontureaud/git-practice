Do Not Get Discouraged

Things may seem easy within the first few lessons, and you may think that everything is going to be a breeze. I am here to tell you that it won't an absolute breeze, but you can and you will get through it. You might have to pace yourself and know when to take a break when things get tough. Taking notes will be your best friend along with printing out the material that is already created for us.

There may come a time where you may think that you want to just stop altogether, don't give up. Believe in yourself, know that in no time you will be a full blown techie! Keeping your eye on your new tech life prize will keep you going.

Questions are encouraged

Everyone at Skillcrush is here to help and wants to help. You couldn't havefound a better program to learn from. Think of it as your personal tech support team, because thats truly what they are, and will give you more support than what's asked for.

So next time you feel doubtful know that you're not alone.
